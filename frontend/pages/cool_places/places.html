Cool Places

Cimarron Park Recreation Center

The Cimarron Park Recreation Center is a lively spot at the heart of Valley Ranch. It offers something for everyone, from fitness and sports programs to classes that bring the community together. The park around it is a peaceful green escape, with trails, sports fields, and playgrounds. Whether you're joining an activity or just having a picnic, the center is a favorite place to relax, have fun, and connect with neighbors.

Valley Ranch Canals

The Valley Ranch Canals give residents a little taste of Venice, right here in Texas. These beautiful waterways are perfect for a relaxing walk, jog, or bike ride. Neatly kept gardens line the winding canals, and you can often see birds and other wildlife along the water. The calm, peaceful feel of the canals adds to Valley Ranch's charm and makes it such a great place to live.
