Cool Places

Cimarron Park Recreation Center

Cimarron Park Recreation Center is a vibrant place located in the heart of Valley Ranch. It has activities for fitness, sports, and classes that unite people. The recreation center is located in a quiet green space with trails, sports fields, and playgrounds. It is a popular place to relax, enjoy, and socialize, regardless of whether you are participating in an activity or having a picnic.

Valley Ranch Canals

Valley Ranch lets you experience a simple taste of Venice, complete with pretty waterways that you can walk, run, or bike on. The winding canals are lined with beautifully maintained gardens, providing a peaceful atmosphere where you might even catch a glimpse of wildlife in the waterways. Mixed with the average, yet amazing suburban feel, living in Valley Ranch is a great experience.

Bruno's Ristorante

Bruno's Ristorante has been a tradition in Valley Ranch for more than 30 years. It offers authentic Italian dishes, fresh seafood, and prime steaks. The restaurant is upscale and has a nice view of the Valley Ranch canal. It is a good place for romance, family, and business events. There is live piano music on Friday and Saturday nights. You can enjoy traditional Northern and Southern Italian dishes and a variety of wine options.
