Fun Facts

Why is it called "Valley Ranch" if there's no valley?

The Original Ridge!

The name comes from the fact that the original ranch land sat below a large ridge, giving it the appearance of a valley. So while there's no traditional valley, the topography created that valley-like effect!

How many Super Bowls did the Dallas Cowboys win while training at Valley Ranch?

3 Super Bowls!

Valley Ranch was home to the legendary Dallas Cowboys from 1985 to 2016. During this era, the team won Super Bowls XXVII, XXVIII, and XXX with the iconic trio of Troy Aikman, Emmitt Smith, and Michael Irvin!

What makes Valley Ranch's population unique in the U.S.?

Incredibly Diverse!

Valley Ranch has a highly diverse population. Some neighborhoods have a higher percentage of residents with Asian and Arab ancestry than almost every other community in the entire United States!

About Us

Welcome to our website, Historical Valley. We are part of the Irving's Visitor's Bureau, and our goal through this website is to convince you to visit a location in our area called Valley Ranch. When you visit, you'll see amazing and fun places that will make you not want to leave. Instead, you'll yearn to learn about its rich history, and lots of other unique things.
