Historical Events

Pre 1970s-

Area was primarily ranch land

Before development, Valley Ranch was largely open ranch land used for agriculture and grazing. Its rural nature reflected the broader landscape of North Texas at the time.

Early 1970s

Interstate 635 Highway Built

The construction of Interstate 635 improved access to the area. This connection made future residential and commercial growth possible.

1974

DFW International Airport Opens

The opening of DFW International Airport boosted regional growth and economic activity. Valley Ranch benefited from its close proximity to the airport.

1983

Flood Control District Established

The flood control district helped manage flooding and protect surrounding land. This made safer development in the area possible.

1985

Dallas Cowboys HQ Opens in Valley Ranch

The Dallas Cowboys moved their headquarters to Valley Ranch, bringing national recognition. This strengthened the area’s identity and economy.

1990-2000

Resedential Development Increase

Valley Ranch experienced rapid residential growth during this period. Schools, neighborhoods, and community facilities were established.

2009

Valley Ranch Islamic Center Founded

The Valley Ranch Islamic Center was founded to serve the growing community. It reflected the area’s increasing cultural diversity.

2020

Major Expansion

Valley Ranch underwent major expansion in housing and commercial development. These additions transformed it into a modern mixed-use community.
